# Typing Game Mechanics: Brainstorming

Here are a few game mechanic concepts that blend typing gameplay with dynamic, LLM-generated interactive storytelling. 

## 1. The Space Shooter (Typing Invaders)
* **Mechanic:** You pilot a spaceship at the bottom of the screen. Enemies (alien ships, asteroids) descend from the top, each carrying a word from the generated story sentence. Typing the word fires a laser that destroys the enemy. The full narrative text is displayed at the bottom of the screen.
* **Path Selection (The Crossroads):** At the end of a story beat or wave, a "hyperspace fork" appears. Two or three distinct wormholes or warp gates appear, each labeled with a choice (e.g., "Investigate distress signal", "Engage cloaking device"). You type the phrase to enter that gate, prompting the LLM to generate the next sequence.

## 2. The Labyrinth Escape (Dynamic Maze)
* **Mechanic:** A top-down or isometric view of an adventurer exploring a shifting labyrinth. The path forward is blocked by doors, traps, or monsters, each labeled with words from the story. You must type the words to unlock the door or defeat the monster and keep moving forward.
* **Path Selection (The Crossroads):** You reach a literal fork in the path. The LLM generates atmospheric descriptions for each route (e.g., "A dark, damp corridor smelling of sulfur" vs. "A brightly lit hall with echoing footsteps"). The player types their choice ("Take the dark corridor") to proceed, and the maze dynamically generates the new rooms.

## 3. The Cinematic Chase (Temple Run / Racing Hybrid)
* **Mechanic:** A continuous runner where you are in a high-speed pursuit (e.g., a cyberpunk motorcycle chase or Indiana Jones running from a boulder). The story text describes the thrilling action. The faster and more accurately you type the text, the faster your character moves, successfully dodging obstacles. If you type too slowly, the pursuer gets closer or you take damage.
* **Path Selection (The Crossroads):** Quick-time events! A sudden obstacle blocks the main path. The LLM presents split-second choices ("Ramp over the truck" or "Swerve into the alley"). The player must rapidly type their choice to execute the maneuver and shape the next part of the story.

## 4. The Cyberpunk Hacker Terminal
* **Mechanic:** The UI is styled like an immersive retro-terminal or a high-tech hacking interface. The story is the log of your infiltration into a secure corporate network. "ICE" (security protocols) attack you as strings of text or encrypted words that you must type to bypass.
* **Path Selection (The Crossroads):** You gain access to a secure node and are presented with system commands as choices (e.g., `> EXECUTE DOWNLOAD_PAYLOAD`, `> INITIATE REACTOR_OVERLOAD`). Typing the command executes the choice, and the LLM describes the immediate consequences.

## 5. The Spellcaster's Duel
* **Mechanic:** You are a wizard facing a magical threat. The story describes the battle unfolding. Words of the story appear as magical runes flying towards you. Typing them correctly "casts spells" to deflect the attacks.
* **Path Selection (The Crossroads):** The enemy changes tactics, or you spot an opportunity. The LLM gives you tactical options ("Cast Fireball at the chandelier", "Use Invisibility to flank"). You type the tactical choice to perform it, changing the flow of the battle.
